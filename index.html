Copper Canyon Dental — Advertiser Agreement

This agreement is between Copper Canyon Dental ("Publisher"), located in Gilbert, Arizona, and the business identified in this application ("Advertiser").

1. Service. Publisher will display Advertiser's 15-second static ad slide on in-office TVs for the rotation frequency specified in the selected plan. Ads run during all patient hours.

2. Term & Cancellation. This agreement is month-to-month. Either party may cancel with 30 days written notice. No partial-month refunds.

3. Content Standards. Publisher reserves the right to reject or remove ad content that conflicts with office values, patient safety, or applicable law. Advertiser warrants they own rights to all submitted content.

4. Payment. Advertiser authorizes automatic monthly billing on the anniversary of sign-up. Failed payments result in ad suspension within 48 hours. Publisher will make one retry before contacting Advertiser.

5. AI-Generated Content. Advertiser approves use of AI tools to generate ad creative on their behalf. Advertiser retains the right to request revisions before their ad goes live.

6. Limitation of Liability. Publisher is not liable for technical outages. Publisher will make commercially reasonable efforts to maintain ad display. Advertiser's sole remedy for outages exceeding 7 days is a prorated credit.

7. Governing Law. This agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Arizona.
